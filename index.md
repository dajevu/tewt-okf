# Atteniv OKF

Welcome to the **Atteniv OKF** knowledge bundle. This is the central entry point for everything you need to understand, use, and contribute to the Atteniv knowledge base.

## About Atteniv

Atteniv is a technology company focused on **attention intelligence** — helping teams understand, measure, and optimize how attention flows across their products, workflows, and customer interactions. Our platform combines behavioral analytics, machine learning, and human-centered design to turn attention data into actionable insight.

Whether you are a new hire getting oriented, a partner integrating with our APIs, or a long-time contributor revisiting a policy, this bundle is designed to give you quick, reliable answers.

## How this bundle is organized

The knowledge base is divided into four kinds of documents:

- **Index** documents — like this one — act as navigation hubs and starting points.
- **Guides** walk you through processes and concepts step by step.
- **Policies** define rules, standards, and expectations that apply across the company.
- **References** provide structured, lookup-style information such as API specs, glossaries, and configuration schemas.
