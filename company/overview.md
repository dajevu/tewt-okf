# Company Overview

Atteniv is a workforce-intelligence company focused on the emerging market for **hybrid-workforce presence tracking** and **tax-nexus intelligence**. As remote and hybrid work become permanent features of the enterprise landscape, organisations face a new class of compliance, payroll, and governance problems created by employees working across many jurisdictions. Atteniv helps companies understand where work is actually happening—and what that means for tax exposure, policy adherence, and operational planning.

## Mission

Atteniv's mission is to give employers a trustworthy, privacy-respecting picture of workforce presence so they can make confident decisions about hiring, mobility, and compliance. We believe that as work decentralises, the systems for tracking and governing it must become more precise, more transparent, and more humane—not less.

## The Problem We Address

Traditional time-and-attendance systems were built for a single office. Modern payroll and HR platforms assume a stable, knowable worksite. Neither assumption holds in a hybrid world:

- Employees may work from home, a co-working space, a client site, or another country—sometimes within the same week.
- Each location can create different tax, immigration, and benefits obligations.
- Employers often lack reliable data about where employees are, while employees fear surveillance and loss of flexibility.
- Regulators are increasingly scrutinising remote-work arrangements and nexus-creating presence.

Atteniv sits at the intersection of these pressures, providing data and tooling that employers, finance teams, and employees can all rely on.

## Market Context

The hybrid-workforce presence tracking and tax-nexus intelligence market has grown out of several converging trends:

1. **Decentralised work** — Post-pandemic, a significant share of knowledge workers operate across multiple locations and jurisdictions.
2. **Tax-nexus enforcement** — States and countries are broadening the triggers that establish corporate tax presence, including days worked by employees within their borders.
3. **Payroll complexity** — Multi-jurisdiction payroll requires accurate location data, withholding adjustments, and audit-ready records.
4. **Policy governance** — Employers need to enforce mobility policies (e.g., day-count limits, approved locations) without resorting to invasive monitoring.
5. **Risk and audit readiness** — Boards and auditors expect defensible data about workforce location.

For related guidance on how Atteniv's products map to these needs, see [Product Overview](../reference/product-overview.md) and [Tax-Nexus Concepts](../guide/tax-nexus-concepts.md).

## Atteniv's Positioning

Atteniv is positioned as the **system of record for workforce presence**—not a surveillance tool, and not a payroll engine, but the connective layer that supplies both with accurate, governed location intelligence. Our differentiation rests on three commitments:

- **Accuracy over surveillance.** We collect presence data through lightweight, employee-controlled check-ins and integrations with existing calendar, VPN, and badge systems rather than continuous device tracking.
- **Compliance by design.** Every data point is captured with an audit trail, jurisdiction tagging, and policy context, so it can feed directly into tax, payroll, and HR workflows.
- **Employee trust.** Visibility flows both ways: employees can see what their employer sees, understand the policies that apply, and manage their own presence records.

## Who We Serve

Atteniv's primary customers are mid-market and enterprise employers with distributed or hybrid workforces, particularly those in regulated industries or with cross-border operations. Typical stakeholders include:

- **Finance and tax leaders** responsible for nexus monitoring and compliance.
- **HR and people-operations teams** managing mobility, policy, and employee experience.
- **Payroll and operations** teams that need clean, jurisdiction-aware inputs.
- **Risk, audit, and legal** functions requiring defensible presence records.

## Where We're Heading

The market for presence tracking and nexus intelligence is still early. Over time we expect presence data to become a foundational input for a wide range of workforce decisions—from hiring and real-estate planning to tax strategy and benefits design. Atteniv's goal is to be the trusted, neutral layer that makes that possible.
